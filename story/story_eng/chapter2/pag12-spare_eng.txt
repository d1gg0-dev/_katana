The battle had been fierce, but the desire for revenge seemed to
be dissipating as he faced the suffering of another.

"I won’t kill you," Takeshi said, his voice resolute yet carrying
a contained sadness. "I’ll leave your fate to be decided by destiny."

Turning, Takeshi began to walk away. Baiken, still fallen and powerless,
watched Takeshi’s figure disappear into the distance. His mind was
consumed by thoughts of his own ambition and the price he had paid
for it.
